Venice
Venice, the capital of northern Italy, is built on more than 100 small islands. With its winding canals, striking architecture, and beautiful bridges, Venice is a popular destination for travel.

1.The Golden House

The palace got its name from the gold detailing in its exterior. Being one city’s oldest palaces, The Golden House is among the most popular Venice attractions.

2.Rialto Bridge

This is the oldest of the 4 bridges built over the city’s Grand Canal. The Rialto Market close to the bridge is among the best places to visit in Venice for shopping.

3.St Mark's Campanile

This bell tower is a part of Basilica di San Marco, but can be considered among significant places to see in Venice in its own right. The tower undergone several transformations in its purpose.
