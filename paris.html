Paris
Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. It's known for its bookshops, including the famed Shakespeare & Company. Family-friendly attractions

1.The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower Locally nicknamed "La dame de fer" (French for "Iron Lady"), it was constructed from 1887 to 1889 by some of France's leading artists and intellectuals for its design, but it has become a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world.

2.The LOUVRE MUSEUM

The LOUVRE MUSEUM is the largest art Museum over the entire world and historic monument in Paris. It is one of the biggest tourist attraction places.

3.Arch of Triumph

Arch of Triumph is the largest monument of its type in the entire world. This ARC was made in the honor of those who fought for the FRANCE, during the Napoleonic Wars.
