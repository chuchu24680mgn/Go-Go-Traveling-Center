New York
New York is a city located in the United State of America. It is a modern city and has a lot of places and things to have fun.

1.The Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty, a gift from France to the United States, for over a hundred years which is one of the most famous place in the world.

2.The Empire State Building

The Empire State Building has made appearances in over 250 films and was named “America’s Favorite Architecture.”

3.The Great Lawn

For naturalists looking to take a break from the big city, relaxing in the Great Lawn or a walk along the extensive paths throughout the park can offer a much needed respite.
